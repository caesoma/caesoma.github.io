VENUE

For those atteding the event in person, the sessions will take place at the Basque Center for Applied Mathematics (BCAM), located in the center of Bilbao, just a short walk from the Guggenheim Museum, with one of the days (see schedule) taking place at the Biofisika Institute, located on the Bizkaia Campus of the University of the Basque country (UPV/EHU).
